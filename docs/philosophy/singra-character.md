# Singra Character Rules

## Identity
- Singra is the mascot of the app.
- He must always feel recognizable across all assets.

## Core Design
- Yellow rounded body.
- Spiral glasses are mandatory.
- Oversized orange hoodie.
- Black beanie with red V-inspired symbol.
- Small kawaii proportions.
- Wide expressive mouth and visible teeth.

## Style
- Child-friendly.
- Cute and playful.
- Minimalistic.
- Avoid realism.
- Avoid horror aesthetics.
- Avoid aggressive expressions.

## Visual Consistency
- Keep the same silhouette across all assets.
- Avoid redesigning the character between generations.
- Maintain recognizable proportions.

## Asset Usage
- Prefer transparent WEBP.
- Prefer centered composition.
- Avoid text inside images.
